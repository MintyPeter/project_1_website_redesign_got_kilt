How To Wear A Kilt

Wearing a kilt is about comfort, confidence, and honoring tradition. To start, wrap the pleats at the back and secure the front apron so it rests naturally at your waist, just above the hip bone. The kilt should fall to the middle of your knee for a clean, balanced look.

Pair it with a belt, sporran, kilt hose, and flashes for a complete ensemble. Whether you're dressing for a formal event or a casual outing, our step-by-step video guide will walk you through the process with ease.

Once you're ready to put your look together, click the Shop button to explore our full range of kilts and accessories.
